# Salesforce CRM Operations

Use this guidance-only skill to design and improve Salesforce operating practices. It does not execute commands or call external APIs.

## When to use

- You need reliable lead-to-opportunity workflows.
- You want cleaner pipeline data and predictable forecasting.
- You need account and activity standards for sales teams.

## Core workflows

### 1) Lead and contact governance

- Define lead qualification criteria and SLA for first touch.
- Standardize routing rules by territory, segment, and product line.
- Require deduplication checks and source attribution consistency.

### 2) Opportunity management

- Define stage entry/exit criteria and required fields per stage.
- Track stalled opportunities with owner follow-up deadlines.
- Enforce next-step and close-date update discipline.

### 3) Forecasting and reporting

- Set forecast cadence with manager review checkpoints.
- Track forecast accuracy by team and segment.
- Use win/loss and cycle-time analysis to tune process quality.

### 4) Account and activity hygiene

- Standardize account ownership and hierarchy governance.
- Define minimum activity logging standards for key deals.
- Run monthly data hygiene audits for missing critical fields.

## Risk controls

- Prevent stage inflation without evidence.
- Avoid duplicate accounts and fragmented contact records.
- Keep compensation-impacting fields under tighter review controls.

## Output format

When asked for help, provide:

- A lead and opportunity governance matrix.
- A pipeline hygiene checklist.
- A forecast review template with exception handling rules.
