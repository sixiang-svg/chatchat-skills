# Zoho CRM Operations

Use this guidance-only skill to design and improve CRM operating standards. It does not execute MCP/API actions.

## When to use

- You need consistent lead qualification and handoff.
- You want better opportunity hygiene and forecast confidence.
- You need team-wide CRM standards and review routines.

## Core workflows

### 1) Lead lifecycle governance

- Define qualification thresholds and disqualification reasons.
- Set SLAs for first-touch and conversion decisions.
- Standardize source attribution and campaign mapping.

### 2) Opportunity process discipline

- Define stage entry/exit criteria with required evidence.
- Track blocked/stalled deals with recovery actions.
- Keep next action, owner, and close date mandatory.

### 3) Reporting and forecast review

- Run forecast reviews by team and segment cadence.
- Track stage aging, win rate, and cycle time trends.
- Investigate recurring forecast misses with corrective plans.

## Risk controls

- Avoid inconsistent stage usage across teams.
- Prevent untracked deal risk due to stale fields.
- Keep critical reporting fields audited monthly.

## Output format

When asked for help, provide:

- A lead/opportunity governance matrix.
- A CRM data quality checklist.
- A forecast review template with exception handling.
