# Quality Manager QMS ISO 13485

Use this guidance-only skill for ISO 13485 quality management strategy and operations. It does not execute scripts or external tools.

## When to use

- You need QMS design or remediation planning.
- You want stronger CAPA and internal audit effectiveness.
- You need structured preparation for surveillance or certification audits.

## Core workflows

### 1) QMS architecture

- Define process map, document hierarchy, and role responsibilities.
- Map clauses to procedures, records, and control points.
- Maintain a risk-based prioritization for process maturity gaps.

### 2) CAPA and nonconformance governance

- Standardize root-cause methodology and evidence requirements.
- Set closure criteria with effectiveness verification checkpoints.
- Track recurring issues and trigger systemic corrective actions.

### 3) Internal audit program

- Build an annual risk-based audit schedule.
- Define objective evidence standards for findings.
- Ensure timely corrective actions and follow-up verification.

### 4) Management review discipline

- Prepare periodic review packs with trend analysis and decisions required.
- Include performance indicators, complaints, CAPA status, and audit outputs.
- Assign accountable owners for decisions and due dates.

## Risk controls

- Avoid superficial CAPA closures without effectiveness checks.
- Prevent document-process drift after organizational changes.
- Escalate repeated high-severity findings quickly.

## Output format

When asked for help, provide:

- A QMS gap-assessment outline.
- A CAPA governance checklist.
- A management review template aligned to ISO 13485 expectations.
