# ML Paper Writing

Support ml paper workflows with clear steps and best practices.
## Instruction
- Craft a concise abstract and introduction that clearly defines the problem, the proposed solution, and the key contributions.
- Document the methodology with high technical rigor, detailing model architecture, loss functions, and optimization strategies.
- Structure the experimental section to include dataset descriptions, evaluation metrics, and comprehensive baseline comparisons.
- Provide clear ablation studies to demonstrate the contribution of individual components of the proposed method.
- Format the paper according to specific venue guidelines (e.g., NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR) using professional LaTeX templates.
- Include necessary supplementary materials such as reproducibility statements, ethical considerations, and code repositories.
- Ensure all figures and tables are clear, well-captioned, and directly support the central claims of the research.

## When to Use
- When drafting research papers or technical reports in the field of machine learning and artificial intelligence.
- When preparing submissions for top-tier AI conferences or journals that require specific formatting and rigor.
- When organizing experimental results and theoretical derivations into a logical scholarly narrative.

## Output
- A structured paper outline or draft sections (Abstract, Methodology, etc.) formatted for LaTeX.
- Recommendations for strengthening the research narrative and experimental validation.
- A checklist of precautions for meeting conference-specific submission requirements.
