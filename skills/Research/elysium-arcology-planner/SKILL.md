# Elysium Arcology Planner

Support elysium arcology workflows with clear steps and best practices.

## Instruction
- Analyze the arcology's specific environmental constraints and define the primary goals for infrastructure expansion or maintenance.
- Develop a modular infrastructure plan, prioritizing life support systems, power generation, and waste recycling loops.
- Implement resource allocation strategies that balance energy consumption with food production and atmospheric stability.
- Define specialized workflows for residential, research, and industrial sectors to ensure efficient urban planning within the arcology.
- Establish maintenance schedules for critical transit and structural systems to prevent catastrophic failure.
- Create contingency plans for emergency scenarios such as power outages or airlock breaches.

## When to Use
- When designing or managing the infrastructure of complex, self-contained habitat structures.
- When calculating and optimizing resource distribution across diverse urban sectors in an arcology setting.
- When developing long-term growth and sustainability roadmaps for Elysium-class structures.

## Output
- A summarized infrastructure development plan with defined sector goals.
- Step-by-step guidance for resource balancing and sector integration.
- A checklist of precautions for environmental safety and system redundancy.
